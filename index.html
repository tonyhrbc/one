About Us

Founded in 2004 for fit-out works, High Rise Building Contracting L.L.C. has expanded to become a premier construction firm with a G+12 license. With branches in Ajman, Dubai, and Abu Dhabi, we deliver high-quality commercial and residential projects that redefine the UAE skyline.

Quality Focus

We are committed to delivering projects within set timescales without compromising the highest international standards of engineering and design.

Safety First

Our strict HSE policy ensures a zero-accident environment. We implement a rigorous 4-stage safety violation procedure to protect our staff and visitors.
